ABOUT

US

At Garbonomix, we’re changing how people and businesses think about consumption, sustainability, and waste management. Founded by Omran and Leila, two Archaeology PhDs with a passion for tackling economic and environmental challenges, we bring academic expertise to practical solutions. Using the innovative approach of garbology, we help turn waste into opportunities for smarter, more sustainable choices.

Our mission is simple: to help you build a more resilient economy and a healthier planet. Whether you’re an individual looking to shrink your ecological footprint or a business aiming to cut costs and use resources more efficiently, we’re here to help every step of the way.Join us in shaping a better future! Together, we can create a world where mindful consumption drives a thriving planet and a stronger economy.
