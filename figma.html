OUR APPROACH: GARBOLOGY

Garbology is a branch of archaeology that explores the behavioral patterns of consumption and waste disposal in modern societies by investigating waste and garbage. This methodology was first practiced by William Rathje in 1973. Garbonomix is the first start-up to apply the garbology methodology in Scandinavia.
